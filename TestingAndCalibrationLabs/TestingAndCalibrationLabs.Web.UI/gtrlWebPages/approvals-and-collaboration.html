Approvals & Collaboration

Approvals: GTRL provide complete Testing & Training solution under one roof for chemical, Chemical, mechanical, construction and electrical materials. It is only Laboratory in Bihar having ISO 9001: 2015 certification & our all equipments are calibrated by NABL. We at our laboratory follow the ISO/IEC 17025 : 2017 standards.

Collaboration: Scientific knowledge has been discovered by the collective intellect of mankind, and we at GTRL believes that it's not possible for single person or organization to take the scientific knowledge at a pace required by the society or human race. So, we are always open and looking for the partners, who are having same ethical and corporate values as ours.

We believe in sustainable growth of the business and society. We will share the profit in return of the synergistic value that we will bring to the joint venture. We work on the philosophy of 'Win/Win' for both the parties.

Thank you for considering GTRL. Please review the information below to determine if a partnership with GTRL makes sense for your company.

  • We are seeking partners with a vision of more than 3 years.
  • We don't work on commission basis.
  • We seek a partner with technical expertise and not only interested in exploiting the market.
  • We would like to have partnership with organizations which are interested in development of protocol and technical expertise on joint basis in addition to serving the local market.

GTRL offers its corporate partners a unique, high-profile opportunity to engage in area of Bihar and Jharkhand which are the fastest growing economic state of India.
